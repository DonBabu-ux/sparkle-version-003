--------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 1 - Foundation Gaps (Priority: CRITICAL)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Seal any missing entry points in the auth flow.

Pages:
  1. Landing.tsx   (views/index.ejs)
     - Public splash page with hero section, features, CTA to Sign Up/Login
     - No auth required
     - API: None (static)

  2. Signup.tsx    (views/auth/signup.ejs)
     - Full registration form: Name, Email, Username, Password, Campus
     - On success: stores JWT, redirects to /dashboard
     - API: POST /api/auth/register

Routes to add in App.tsx:
  /         --> Landing.tsx (public)
  /signup   --> Signup.tsx  (public)
